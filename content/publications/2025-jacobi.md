The Double Bracket Structure is a variational bracket framework. This research report presents various details of calculus that have been required to derive the results of a main paper, submitted to a peer-reviewed journal. First, the structure is derived for a uni-dimensional small strain generalized standard material, with a general quadratic dissipation potential.
